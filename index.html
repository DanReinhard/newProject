a summary


Part One: Normal...-ish

Born in the summer of '93, to my parents--one a Californian beach bum turned
computer scientist and the other an artist turned stay-at-home mother--in the
in the city of Orem, UT, I feel safe to say that my life has been ordinary.
Very, extremely, painfully ordinary.

I grew up with nothing but sisters for the first 14 years of my life. There is
a little brother in my life now but, unfortunately for him, he grew up not with two parents
but with seven. He has almost been more like a nephew to me but I still call him brother.

Ordinary things to talk about but again I have been a rather ordinary person most my life

My interests include piano, stained glass, whistling (to the point of annoyance or so my
girlfriend tells me), screaming into the void, and touching every pole as I walk down the street.
Are you bored yet...No? Well then. Onto round two. My passion, yes there is a difference
between interest and passion, is to make people laugh. You know you've done something right when you are able
to make the whole room hold their sides as if their insides were falling out. If their insides are
falling out, I suspect you've done something wrong. 

Note: Should you not find this humorous, I suggest acquiring five cents and purchasing some taste.>_<
